Our Programs &
Initiatives

Education

Back to School Initiative

Providing school fees, uniforms, and learning materials to children from low-income families in Arusha. We believe education is the most powerful tool for breaking the poverty cycle.

Supported 850+ students since 2021
💪
Women's Power

Women Rise Program

Equipping women with vocational skills, business mentorship, and micro-financing opportunities to become financially independent and community leaders in their own right.

300+ women trained in business skills
🏥
Health

Community Health Outreach

Free health camps, sanitation drives, and hygiene education in underserved communities. Partnering with local clinics to ensure basic healthcare is never out of reach.

5,000+ beneficiaries in health camps
🌟
Youth

Youth Champions Club

A mentorship and leadership club for teenagers, building confidence, communication skills, and social responsibility. Young people learn to be champions in their own communities.

200+ youth enrolled annually
🍽️
Food Security

Hunger Relief Program

Monthly food drives and community kitchens providing nutritious meals to orphaned children, elderly individuals, and families facing extreme food insecurity.

400+ families fed every month

Scola Sumary

Founder & Executive Director

Born and raised in Tanzania, Scola witnessed firsthand how poverty, inequality, and lack of opportunity silenced the voices of young people and women in her community. Fuelled by compassion and a burning belief that change is possible, she founded Champions for Changes to ensure that every person has the chance to reach their full potential.

"I started this organisation because I refused to accept that where you are born should determine how far you go."

2020
Founded
Our Story

Built on Compassion. Driven by Purpose.

Champions for Changes was founded in 2019 in Arusha, Tanzania, with a simple but powerful idea: communities thrive when individuals within them are lifted up with dignity, opportunity, and support.

From a small community initiative, we have grown into a recognised NGO operating across 6 communities in northern Tanzania, running programs in education, women's empowerment, healthcare, and youth development.

❤️
Compassion First Every action we take is rooted in genuine care for the people we serve.
🔬
Dignity & Respect We treat every individual as a full human being with unlimited potential.
🌱
Sustainable Change We invest in long-term transformation, not short-term fixes.
🤲
